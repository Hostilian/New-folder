Introduction

The main objective of the course is to acquire knowledge and practical skills in the use of relational database technology for the creation, maintenance and practical operation of database-based information security of enterprises and institutions.  This knowledge will enable you to analyse, design and subsequently implement the required data repositories (databases) based on the specifications of their principals in accordance with the needs of practice. The organisation of the course and exam

Each student will get their own schema in the database server db.kii.pef.czu.cz 

They will connect to the schema and here they will have full rights to create their own data model.

Each student will prepare a term paper independently. This thesis will contain at least 7 entities (simple binding entity does not count). They will populate these entities with data. 

Above the data model, the student will create 10 queries in relational algebra (abbreviated RA) and 24 SQL (10 are shared with RA) queries see: term paper Boat delivery Ing. Michal Valenta, Ph.D.

The semester paper will be handed in at the last res. penultimate exercises to his/her practitioner. He/she will mark it with 1,2,3,4 marks. 

All RA and SQL queries will be stored under the student account in the DBS portal. 

If the student obtains at least grade 3 he will be allowed to write a short test, verifying his knowledge of SQL, RA, ERD, Data Modeling and theory. 

The overall grade is the average of the test grade and the term paper grade. If the student fails at any point, he/she has to repeat the course.
