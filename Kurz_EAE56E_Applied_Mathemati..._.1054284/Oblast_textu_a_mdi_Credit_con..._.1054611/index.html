Credit conditions

Credit in case of repetition of the subject or course is not recognized.

1.    Attendance at seminars, only two absences are allowed in the case of full-time teaching.

2.    Gain at least 50% of points from the weighted sum of the results of the following tests

0,5*Self-tests + 0,5*BigCreditTest

a.     Successful passing of Self-tests in Moodle. They are filled in in the Moodle environment as part of home preparation. Access to the Self-tests is granted to students after fulfilling the access conditions listed for each Self-test. Each Self-test can be completed a maximum of 3 times, the best attempt counts. You will find the individual self-tests at the end of the last topic for which the self-test is ready. All self-test must be completed.

b.    Successful completion of the Big Credit Test in Moodle. The big test will be announced for all students at once - a mandatory date. We suppose the 9th week of the semester.
