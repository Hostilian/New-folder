Fairness and equal conditions, Dress code

Obsah stránky

In order to ensure fairness and equal conditions for all the students sitting for an exam, the following rules are applied for the written part of the exam:


Students are required to prove their identity by means of an identity card, driving license or passport. If the student fails to submit any of these documents, he/she will not be allowed to take the exam.

 

During the exam, the student can only use plain paper, pens, a ruler, non-programmable calculator, and, if explicitly permitted, any other documents which must not contain additional comments. Using any other material, tools or notes is strictly forbidden and will result in an immediate exclusion of the student from the exam and his/her evaluation will be marked as "failed".

Additionally, the student is required to turn off his/her mobile phone or smart watch and store them in a handbag, purse, or backpack, etc.  If these devices are not switched off and not stored in a bag, such behaviorwill be classified as cheating and will result in an immediate termination of the student’s exam which will then be classified as "failed".


Cheating students will be called for explanation to the Disciplinary Committee of the Faculty of Economics and Management, University of Life Sciences Prague.

 

Please note that during the oral exam the dress code is requested (smart and clean clothes).
