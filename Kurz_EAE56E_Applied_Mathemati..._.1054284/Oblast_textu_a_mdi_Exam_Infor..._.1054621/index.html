Exam Information

The exam test in Moodle has the form of an open book electronic test with various types of questions and forms of answers (multiple choice, free answer, calculation). Each test represents a solution to consecutive or related problems of a given story, company, person, etc. You will use several discussed models with calculation, always with analysis of results and theoretical knowledge of the models.

You may use written materials when solving tasks, but you may not borrow them from each other; you have to work independently.

It is necessary to receive at least 60% of the points. No exceptions, this is an open book test.

Each of you has the right to see the test results.

Oral exam condition – at least 60% of points from the exam test.

Repetition of exam

You have three attempts of the exam (one regular and two repetitions). Each starts with the exam test in Moodle, and continues with an oral examination.
