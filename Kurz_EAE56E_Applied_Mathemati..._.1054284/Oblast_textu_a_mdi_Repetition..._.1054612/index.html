Repetition of credit

If you have participated in all Self-tests and Big Credit Test, and if you fail in them, you must pass the Resit Credit Test in Moodle. The resit credit test in the scope of the whole semester will take place in the 1st week of the examination period; you have to register for this test in the Study Information System. The condition for successful completion is same as for credit: gain at least 50% of 0,5*Self-tests + 0,5*CreditTest.
