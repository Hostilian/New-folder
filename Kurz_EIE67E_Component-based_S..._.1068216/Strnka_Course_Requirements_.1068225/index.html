Obsah stránky

To successfully pass this course, you need to:

* be preliminarily credited for the semester
* pass the fexam

To be preliminarily credited, you need to:

* pick and announce a topic of the exam project
* submit the exam project to the Moodle system by the deadline
* attend the lectures



To sit the exam, you need to:

* be credited from the semester (you cannot take the exam without the credit)

The exam consists of:

* oral examination from the course theory topics; student can decide to skip the oral examination and accept a proposed grade from the semestral credit test instead
* presenting and defending your semestral project to prove that you understand what you created (eg. you have to be able to explain what every piece of the source code does, your choice of approach etc.)
